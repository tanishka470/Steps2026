Call for Full Papers

Call For Papers are invited in the form of oral and poster presentations. All those desirous of participating and/or presenting in the conference are requested to complete the online Call For Paper submission. Authors are requested to send Call For Paper in the prescribed format as per template. Best posters will be awarded by the conference.

Call For Papers for Oral and Poster presentations should be in Arial Narrow with in A4 format. The title should be in bold with font size of 14 followed by authors name(s) in the next line, and affiliation in the subsequent line(s) as central alignment. This should follow the Email ID of the presenting author. The presenting author should be underlined. The text should follow as single space with font size of 10. References should be placed at the bottom.

STEPS 2025

The IEEE Conference on Sustainable Technologies for Power and Energy Systems (STEPS 2025) is an exceptional gathering of minds dedicated to advancing the field of power and energy systems. With a diverse program featuring keynote speeches, technical paper presentations, panel discussions, and workshops, STEPS 2025 covers a wide spectrum of topics ranging from renewable energy integration to cyber security in energy systems.

Tracks

The conference seeks papers in all aspects of Electrical Power, Green Energy, Power Electronic, and Control Systems. Specifically, it covers (but not limited to) the following Topics:

  • Green Energy Technologies and energy storage systems.

  • Plug-in and Hybrid electric vehicles.

  • Smart Grid and micro-grid Technologies.

  • Power Electronics and Industrial power drives.

  • Industrial Electronics.

  • Modern Control system and Intelligent Systems.

  • Other related Topics.

STEPS 2026

The IEEE Conference on Sustainable Technologies for Power and Energy Systems (STEPS 2026) is an exceptional gathering of minds dedicated to advancing the field of power and energy systems. With a diverse program featuring keynote speeches, technical paper presentations, panel discussions, and workshops, STEPS 2026 covers a wide spectrum of topics ranging from renewable energy integration to cyber security in energy systems.
